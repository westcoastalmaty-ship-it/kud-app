Privacy Policy for "Kuda Ukhodyat Dengi — Expense Tracker"

Application ID: com.kudaukhodyatdengi.app
Last updated: 20 July 2026

Summary

The app helps you track your spending. To record a purchase automatically, it recognises the confirmation screen of a completed payment inside your banking app.

The text of that screen is sent to the cloud for recognition and is not retained there. The resulting expense records are stored only on your device.

We do not read passwords, one-time codes or private messages. We do not share your data with advertisers and do not use it for advertising.

1. Who processes your data

The developer of the app is westtel, based in Almaty, Republic of Kazakhstan.

Privacy contact: westcoastalmaty@gmail.com

2. How automatic tracking works

With your explicit consent, the app uses the Android Accessibility Service. Consent is requested on a dedicated screen before the feature is enabled, and you may decline — in that case the app continues to work in manual entry mode.

What the app reads

  • The contents of banking and payment app screens at the moment a completed transaction result is displayed.
  • From those contents it extracts the transaction amount, the merchant or recipient name, the date and the spending category.

What the app does not read

  • Password, PIN and login entry screens — these are filtered out on the device and are not processed.
  • One-time confirmation codes from SMS.
  • Messenger conversations, email contents, notes, or any app unrelated to payments.
  • Card credentials or banking app login details.

The app does not act on your behalf: it does not tap buttons, does not initiate transfers and does not modify anything inside banking apps.

3. Data transmitted for processing

To recognise a transaction and determine its spending category, the text of the payment screen is transmitted over an encrypted connection to a processing backend and from there to a recognition service.

Parties involved in processing

  • Cloudflare, Inc. — the infrastructure through which the request passes.
  • Anthropic PBC — the service that recognises the transaction text and determines its category.

These companies act solely as processors on our instructions. The transmitted text is used only to produce a response to that specific request.

The transmitted screen text is not stored in any database, is not linked to your name or phone number, and is not used to train models. Technical logs are anonymised and kept in volatile memory for a limited time.

4. Data stored on your device

Expense records, categories, limits, tags and settings are stored in a local database on your phone. We have no access to that database.

You may optionally protect the app with a PIN code. It is stored on the device as an irreversible hash.

5. Technical information about app operation

So that automatic tracking does not stop working without you noticing, the app periodically sends a service signal about its own state. It contains:

  • a device identifier provided by the Android system;
  • the device model and manufacturer, and the Android version;
  • the app version and the state of the automatic tracking service.

This information contains no transaction amounts, no merchant names and no details of your purchases. It is used solely for fault diagnostics.

The app does not collect location data, contacts, photos or files. No advertising identifiers are used and the app contains no advertising.

6. Subscriptions and payment

Paid plans are purchased through Google Play. Payment is processed by Google — your card details never reach the app and are not passed to us.

The app receives from Google only the subscription status required to unlock paid features.

7. Your rights

  • Disable automatic tracking. Revoke the Accessibility permission in your system settings — data transmission stops immediately.
  • Delete your data. Uninstalling the app erases all local records. To delete technical service information, use the data deletion page or contact us.
  • Request information. You may ask what data relating to your installation we hold by writing to the address above.

We respond to requests within 30 days.

8. Children

The app is intended for users aged 18 and over and is not directed at children. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

9. Changes to this policy

When this policy changes, we update the date shown at the top of this page. Material changes affecting data processing are communicated to users inside the app.
